# Boss Dreamcallers

Boss dreamcallers are special opponents encountered during quests. Minibosses
appear at the 4th battle, and final bosses appear at the 7th battle. Each boss
has a single dreamcaller ability that defines their archetype.

## Minibosses

### 1. Pyrra, Ember Dancer

**Archetype:** Aggro/Burn — cheap threats that directly score points.

**Ability:** Whenever you play a character that costs 2 or less energy, kindle
1\.

**Deck:** Floods the board with cheap low-cost characters and events that
directly grant victory points, aiming to win before the opponent stabilizes.

### 2. Sylvara, Chorus Weaver

**Archetype:** Go Wide — flood the battlefield with cheap characters and pump
them up.

**Ability:** Whenever you materialize a non-figment character, materialize a
Celestial figment.

**Deck:** Low-cost characters and events that grant spark to all characters
simultaneously, quickly overwhelming with board-wide pressure.

### 3. Thornroot, Grove-Warden

**Archetype:** Spirit Animal Tribal — ramp into expensive threats via cheap
spirit animals.

**Ability:** Each Spirit Animal you control increases your energy production by
1\.

**Deck:** Cheap Spirit Animal characters that generate energy, ramping into
expensive high-spark threats far earlier than normal.

### 4. Arakos, the Iron Banner

**Archetype:** Warrior Tribal — synergistic lords that pump up all warriors.

**Ability:** Your Warrior characters have +2 spark.

**Deck:** Warriors at every cost with tribal synergy effects, creating a board
where each individual warrior is a meaningful threat.

### 5. Vesper, Twilight Arbiter

**Archetype:** Midrange — efficient threats backed up by removal and hand
disruption.

**Ability:** Whenever you dissolve an enemy character, draw a card.

**Deck:** Efficient mid-cost characters backed by removal events and effects
that force the opponent to discard, grinding out card advantage.

### 6. Mara, Whisper of the Lost

**Archetype:** Self-Discard — rewards for discarding your own cards.

**Ability:** Whenever you discard a card from your hand, materialize a Shadow
figment.

**Deck:** Cards with discard costs, effects that force self-discard, and payoffs
for having few cards in hand, converting card disadvantage into board presence.

### 7. Lord Ashworth, the Pale Collector

**Archetype:** Aristocrats — abandon your own characters for value.

**Ability:** Whenever you abandon a character, gain 1 point.

**Deck:** Cheap characters with materialized triggers and cards that require
abandoning as a cost, turning a stream of disposable characters into victory
points.

### 8. Luminos, Dream Sculptor

**Archetype:** Figments — rewards for creating and buffing figments.

**Ability:** Your figments have +2 spark.

**Deck:** Cards that create multiple figments of all types and events that buff
all characters, turning normally modest figment tokens into serious threats.

### 9. Solenne, the Flickering Star

**Archetype:** Blink — banish your characters and re-materialize them to
re-trigger abilities.

**Ability:** 1 energy: Banish target character you control until your next main
phase.

**Deck:** Characters with powerful materialized triggers and judgment abilities
that benefit from repeated re-entry to the battlefield.

### 10. Aegis, the Bastion

**Archetype:** Tap-out Control — proactive big threats with protection.

**Ability:** Characters you control with spark 4 or more cannot be dissolved.

**Deck:** Expensive high-spark characters with protective static abilities and
energy ramp, building an impenetrable wall of unkillable threats.

## Final Bosses

### 11. Nihil, the Silence Between

**Archetype:** Draw-Go Control — counterspells, sweepers, and removal.

**Ability:** Whenever your opponent plays a card, draw a card.

**Deck:** Prevent effects, sweeper events that dissolve all characters, spot
removal, and a handful of high-cost finisher characters that close the game once
the opponent is out of resources.

### 12. Keth, the Binding Dark

**Archetype:** Prison — restrict the opponent's actions and resources until you
win.

**Ability:** Your opponent's cards cost 1 more energy to play.

**Deck:** Cards that further restrict the opponent's energy production, hand
size, and available actions, slowly strangling the opponent while chipping away
at victory points.

### 13. Yune, Weaver of Ends

**Archetype:** Combo-Control — play reactively and then assemble a 3-card combo
to win.

**Ability:** At the start of your turn, foresee 3.

**Deck:** Reactive removal, prevention, and card draw to stall the game while
digging through the deck for three specific combo characters that win the game
when all three are on the battlefield simultaneously.

### 14. Zephyra, Tempest Unbound

**Archetype:** Spellslinger/Storm — play a huge turn with dozens of events to
win.

**Ability:** Whenever you play an event, gain 1 energy.

**Deck:** Cheap events that draw cards and generate resources, building toward
explosive turns where dozens of events chain together, each refunding its own
cost.

### 15. Morwen, the Pale Dreamer

**Archetype:** Reanimator — discard large characters and bring them back from
the void.

**Ability:** At the start of your turn, materialize the character in your void
with the highest spark.

**Deck:** Massive high-spark characters, self-discard and self-mill effects to
fill the void, and removal to stall until the reanimation engine takes over.

### 16. Dusk, Veil-Render

**Archetype:** Cheat-into-Play — bypass energy requirements on expensive cards.

**Ability:** Characters you play cost 3 less energy (minimum 1).

**Deck:** Overpowered high-cost characters with devastating abilities that are
normally balanced by their steep energy cost, deployed multiple turns ahead of
schedule.

### 17. Echomaw, Memory Devourer

**Archetype:** Self-Mill — value from filling up your own void.

**Ability:** Judgment: Gain 1 point for every 5 cards in your void.

**Deck:** Self-mill effects via foresee, cards that move directly from deck to
void, and void-matters payoffs that grow stronger as the void fills up.

### 18. Nyx, the Midnight Whisper

**Archetype:** Flash — rewards for playing at fast speed during the opponent's
turn.

**Ability:** Whenever you play a fast card during your opponent's turn, draw a
card.

**Deck:** Fast characters that can be materialized at instant speed, fast events
with disruptive effects, and payoffs for controlling the tempo of the opponent's
turn.

### 19. Ashara, the Relentless Tide

**Archetype:** Tempo — a few resilient threats backed up by answers to
everything the opponent does.

**Ability:** Whenever you play an event that targets an enemy character,
materialize a Radiant figment.

**Deck:** Efficient threats with self-protection, bounce and removal events that
simultaneously answer the opponent's board while developing your own.

### 20. Titanus, the Living Monolith

**Archetype:** Voltron/Go Tall — pile buffs and protection onto one character.

**Ability:** Your leftmost character has +5 spark and cannot be dissolved.

**Deck:** Buff events that add spark to a single target, protection effects, and
cards that ensure one towering character survives to dominate every judgment
phase.
