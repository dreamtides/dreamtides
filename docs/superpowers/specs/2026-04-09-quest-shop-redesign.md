# Quest Shop-Focused Redesign

## Overview

Redesign the quest prototype's early game to reduce decision overwhelm. Remove
starting tide selection, use a fixed starter deck, make shops the primary card
acquisition method, and reset essence between dreamscapes.

## Starting Flow

Remove the QuestStartScreen tide selection entirely. The player goes straight
into their first dreamscape with a fixed starter deck and 0 tide crystals.

### Fixed Starter Deck (30 cards)

All cards from the 10-card Starter pool, no tide or neutral extras:

| Card                  | Cost | Type      | Copies |
|-----------------------|------|-----------|--------|
| Glimpse of What Was   | 1    | Event     | 4      |
| Worlds Await          | 1    | Event     | 3      |
| Nocturne Strummer     | 2    | Character | 4      |
| Flashpoint Detonation | 2    | Event     | 2      |
| Sign of Arrival       | 2    | Event     | 2      |
| Final Witness         | 3    | Character | 3      |

| Marked Direwolf       | 4    | Character | 4      |
| Runebound Champion    | 5    | Character | 3      |
| Wildflower Colossus   | 6    | Character | 2      |

Curve is weighted toward cheap cards for functional opening hands.

## First Dreamscape (Level 0)

### Site Composition

Sites in order: DreamcallerDraft, LootPack, LootPack, CardShop, PackShop,
Battle.

No other sites (no Forge, Transfiguration, Provisioner, etc.).

### Tide Selection

A single random tide is chosen for the dreamscape. Both loot packs are from that
tide. Shops draw from that tide and its two neighbors (the 3-tide neighborhood).
For example, if the dreamscape tide is Ignite, shops favor Ignite/Arc/Pact.

- Card Shop: cards weighted toward the 3-tide neighborhood
- Pack Shop: tide packs drawn from the neighborhood; special packs remain
  thematically random

## Economy

### New Defaults

| Parameter          | Old     | New     |
|--------------------|---------|---------|
| Starting essence   | 250     | 400     |
| Card price range   | 50-100  | 30-70   |
| Reroll base        | 40      | 10      |
| Reroll increment   | 20      | 5       |
| Pack price (tide)  | 100     | 75      |
| Pack price (special) | 100-125 | 75-100 |

### Essence Loss Between Dreamscapes

All unspent essence is lost when transitioning to a new dreamscape. Players
start each new dreamscape with 0 essence and build up via battle rewards and
essence sites.

### Unspent Essence Warning

When the player initiates a battle with >= 30 essence (enough to buy a card),
show a confirmation dialog:

> "You have X essence remaining. Unspent essence is lost after this battle.
> Continue?"

This warning is shown once per run, then permanently suppressed for that run.

## Subsequent Dreamscapes (Level 1+)

### Shop Presence

Every dreamscape includes at least a Card Shop and a Pack Shop, in addition to
whatever other sites are generated by the existing composition logic.

### Shop Tide Allocation

Use the existing weighting logic for level 1+: favor player's dominant tides
with some exploration weight. The 3-tide neighborhood anchor is specific to
dreamscape 1 only.

## Unchanged Systems

The following systems are not modified by this redesign:

- Atlas structure and navigation
- Site generation for non-shop sites (level 1+)
- Dreamsigns and dreamsign sites
- Tide crystals (though player starts with 0)
- Ante system
- Forge, Transfiguration, and other non-shop sites
- Battle rewards (150 + level * 50)
- Deck editor (free card removal remains available)
